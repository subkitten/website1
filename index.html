August Browning and the champions he's created and developed in League of Legends

*Fun Facts and Accomplishments*

  • Joined Riot Games in 2012 as soon as he got out of college
  • August went through a few different gamertags over the years (groovylord, jinxylord) before settling on his given name and becoming Riot August
  • Has designed and developed champions such as Senna, Jhin, Jinx, and Zeri
  • He's got a little habit of giving his champs 3-attack passives, so he gave Jhin a 4-attack passive
  • A quote from August about Champion design and development in League of Legends: "One fun thing about design is that there are different ways to approach it. I try to ask myself what the character is like and how I want it to feel. For example, with Jinx I knew I wanted to do a crazy, very unstable maniac. With Vi I was thinking of something more aggressive, punchy, that made you think that attack is the best defense and with Senna it was about the artists who created her amazing weapon."
